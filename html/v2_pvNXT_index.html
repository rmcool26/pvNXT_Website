The 6 Portals

Each portal solves a specific problem. Together, they eliminate the gaps between them.

01

Studio

Live

From rooftop to proposal, in one workspace.

What is this portal?

A single EPC workspace where your team goes from raw site data to a proposal-ready design — layout, shadow analysis, BOQ, and report, all created in one place without switching tools.

Who is it for?

EPC designers, sales teams, and solar businesses. Currently used by 4+ EPCs.

Main Features

  • →Solar layout creation
  • →Shadow and roof analysis
  • →BOQ generation
  • →Reports
  • →Proposal-ready output
  • →Project tracking

Studio Portal Visual

Before

  • Manual CAD drawings taking 2–3 days
  • Sizing errors requiring multiple revisions
  • Shadow analysis done separately in another tool

After

  • Designs generated in under 15 minutes
  • Accurate first time, no rework
  • Shadow analysis built into the design flow

Outcome You Get

Design time reduced from days to minutes. Fewer errors, faster turnaround, and designs that feed directly into proposals.

How It Makes the Overall Process Faster

Eliminates the design bottleneck that delays every downstream step. Proposal and project teams get accurate data without waiting.

Field App

Internal

One visit, complete site capture.

What is this portal?

A field capture app that ensures every site visit produces a complete handoff — site data, roof details, keepouts, measurements, photos, and structured notes, all captured in one go.

Who is it for?

Field survey teams who visit sites and need to pass clean data to the design team.

Main Features

  • →Site data capture
  • →Roof details and keepouts
  • →Measurements
  • →Photos
  • →Structured handoff notes

Field App Visual

Before

  • Site visits gave incomplete handoffs
  • Design team had to ask for missing details later
  • Multiple visits sometimes needed to gather everything

After

  • One visit captures site data, roof details, keepouts, measurements, photos, and handoff notes
  • Design team gets everything they need the first time
  • Proposals move faster because data is complete

Outcome You Get

A cleaner handoff in one visit. Fewer missing details, fewer revisits, and proposals that move faster because the data is complete.

How It Makes the Overall Process Faster

One clean visit replaces multiple back-and-forths. Design team gets structured data on day one — no waiting for missing information to come through.

SCADA

In progress

Who is it for?

EPC managers, plant owners, field techs, and O&M owners. Currently used for audits and by Aston Greens EPC.

Main Features

  • →Role-based views (Manager, Owner, Tech, O&M)
  • →Live alerts
  • →Work orders and assignment
  • →Activity history
  • →Plant-level monitoring

What Changes With a Connected System

1

The customer starts the flow

A homeowner checks feasibility and gets a quote through Connect. When they proceed, the data moves straight to Studio — no one has to re-enter anything.

2

Design to proposal, no manual step

Studio produces the layout, BOQ, and report in one workspace. Proposal auto-generates from that data — no manual creation, no copy-paste, no waiting.

3

Site execution stays visible

Field App captures complete site data in one visit and feeds it to Studio. Go gives EPC real-time progress with daily photo proofs and delay reasons — no more chasing installers on WhatsApp.

4

Operations stay accountable

SCADA gives each stakeholder the right view — live alerts, assigned work orders, and full activity history. Every issue has a clear owner, so nothing gets dropped.

5

The impact is cumulative

Each portal removes its own manual steps. The connections between them remove the handoff delays. Together, the overall project timeline shrinks significantly.
