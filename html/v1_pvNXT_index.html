The 6 Portals

Each portal solves a specific problem. Together, they eliminate the gaps between them.

01

Studio

Design in Minutes

What is this portal?

A cloud-based design tool that generates accurate solar layouts, shadow analysis, and system sizing instantly—no CAD software required.

Who is it for?

Design engineers, sales engineers, and EPC teams who need to produce accurate designs quickly.

Main Features

  • →Auto-layout generation from satellite imagery
  • →Shadow analysis and obstruction mapping
  • →Accurate system sizing and equipment selection
  • →Export-ready design reports
  • →Single-click report generation

Studio Portal Visual

Before

  • Manual CAD drawings taking 2–3 days
  • Sizing errors requiring multiple revisions
  • Shadow analysis done separately

After

  • Designs generated in under 15 minutes
  • Accurate first time, no rework
  • Shadow analysis built into the flow

Outcome You Get

Design time reduced from days to minutes. Fewer errors, faster turnaround, and designs that feed directly into proposals.

How It Makes the Overall Process Faster

Eliminates the design bottleneck that delays every downstream step. Proposal and project teams get accurate data instantly.

Main Features

  • →Pipeline visibility with stage tracking
  • →Conversion analytics and reporting

  • Leads scattered across WhatsApp and spreadsheets

After

  • →Vendor coordination and material tracking

05

Field App

Execution, Digitized

What is this portal?

A mobile-first app for on-site teams to execute tasks, document progress, and report issues—without paper and without delays.

Who is it for?

Site engineers, installation teams, and QA inspectors working on solar project sites.

Main Features

  • →Digital checklists for every installation step
  • →Photo documentation with GPS tagging
  • →Issue logging and escalation
  • →Real-time sync with the Go portal
  • →Offline capability for remote sites

Field App Visual

Before

  • Paper checklists that get lost or damaged
  • Progress reported days after the fact
  • No documentation trail for disputes

After

  • Digital execution, zero paper
  • Instant reporting back to project managers
  • Complete audit trail with photos and timestamps

Outcome You Get

Zero paper on site, full traceability, and faster handovers. Every action is documented and timestamped.

How It Makes the Overall Process Faster

Site data flows back to the Go portal in real time. Project managers see progress as it happens, not days later.

  • →Weather correlation analysis
  • →Automated reporting for stakeholders
