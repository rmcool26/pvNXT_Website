How It Works

From Inquiry to Energy.

A seamless journey across the Terranxt ecosystem — physical and digital working together.

Step 01

Land & Site Assessment

Renxt identifies and assesses suitable land or rooftop space. Solar irradiance, shadow analysis, grid proximity, and regulatory checks — all done upfront.

Powered by Renxt
Step 02

System Design

pvNXT Studio generates precise system layouts, equipment selection, and energy yield projections. What used to take days now takes minutes.

Powered by pvNXT Studio
Step 03

Proposal & Agreement

Professional proposals are generated automatically with accurate pricing, system specs, and financial returns. Consumers review and approve — all digitally.

Powered by pvNXT Proposal
Step 04

Physical Installation

Terranxt's field teams execute the installation with real-time progress tracking via the Field App. Photo documentation, quality checkpoints, and timeline adherence — all digital.

Powered by pvNXT Field App
Step 05

Monitoring & Returns

SCADA monitors every system in real-time. For investors, Artha provides transparent performance data and structured returns — the full loop from capital to energy to income.

Powered by SCADA + Artha
