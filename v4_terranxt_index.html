About Terranxt

One company. Three platforms.
One mission.

Terranxt was born out of IIT Delhi's research ecosystem with a clear premise: India's solar transition needs connected infrastructure, not fragmented tools.

We built three complementary platforms — pvNXT for operations and data, Artha for finance, and Renxt for lifecycle sustainability — each powerful alone, transformative together.

Renxt by Terranxt

Closing the loop on
solar sustainability.

Renxt handles what happens after installation — panel lifecycle management, decommissioning, and responsible recycling. Because real sustainability doesn't end at commissioning.

  • End-of-life panel collection and logistics
  • Certified recycling with material recovery tracking
  • Circular economy reporting and ESG compliance
