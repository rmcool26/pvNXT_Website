## 1. PROJECT OVERVIEW

Terranxt is the parent company behind the pvNXT ecosystem. It uniquely combines **physical solar project execution** with **world-class digital intelligence**.

- **pvNXT** is the flagship AI + GIS platform that automates the entire solar project lifecycle with "Zero Physical Site Visits".
- The ecosystem also includes **ArthaNxt** (investment in tokenized green assets) and **Renxt** (land/real estate for solar).
- Terranxt delivers end-to-end solutions — from finding land (Renxt) → investing (Artha) → automated design & execution (pvNXT + physical delivery).

The new website must:
- Clearly establish **Terranxt** as the parent company
- Make **pvNXT** the hero digital product with strong "Zero Physical Site Visits" positioning
- Prominently feature **Artha** as the gateway for users who want to **invest in solar**
- Feature **Renxt** for real estate/land opportunities
- Feel premium, technical, clean, and trustworthy
- Drive two main outcomes: **Investment** and **Platform Adoption**

## 5. DESIGN SYSTEM (Premium Corporate)

**Primary Color:** #069fb1 (Teal) — Main accent for buttons, links, icons, and highlights.

**Typography (Corporate & Restrained):**
- Headings: Satoshi or Neue Haas Grotesk (500–600 weight)
- Body: IBM Plex Sans or Satoshi (400–500)
- Moderate weights only — clean and professional

**Color Palette:**
- Backgrounds: Clean white + subtle off-white
- Text: Deep charcoal (#0F1419)
- Accent: #069fb1 (Teal)
- Supporting: Soft grays and minimal solar-inspired accents

**Style:**
- Clean, modern, technical corporate (inspired by the provided pvNXT screenshots)
- Generous whitespace
- Asymmetric layouts in key sections
- Sharp edges, minimal radius
- High-quality aerial, drone, GIS, and professional imagery

**Motion:**
- Restrained and purposeful (corporate level)
- GSAP scroll pinning for "How It Works" and ecosystem sections
- Subtle hover states
- No bouncy or excessive animations
