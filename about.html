About pvNXT

Building the digital backbone for solar decisions.

pvNXT is a connected solar workflow suite by Terranxt — an IIT Delhi-FITT incubated deep-tech startup. Seven products, one shared data layer, zero friction.

The Problem

Solar shouldn't be this hard.

For consumers

Jargon-heavy quotes, no visibility into installation, and guesswork on ROI. Going solar feels like a leap of faith.

For EPCs

Manual CAD drawings, fragmented tools, and design bottlenecks that delay every downstream step.

For installers

Whiteboard tracking, delayed updates, and no visibility across multiple sites. Field and office work in silos.

For O&M teams

Reactive checking, unclear issue ownership, and no traceable history. Operations lack accountability.

The Solution

One workflow. Seven products. Zero friction.

pvNXT connects every step of the solar workflow on one shared data layer. From satellite modeling to plant monitoring, data flows automatically — no manual handoffs, no repeated site visits.
