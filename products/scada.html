Overview

What is SCADA Monitoring?

pvNXT SCADA Monitoring brings accountability to solar plant operations. Role-based dashboards show exactly what each user needs to see. Live alerts flag issues instantly. Work orders assign ownership. Activity history tracks every action. From reactive checking to proactive operations — every alert has an owner.

What it does

Operations, accountable.

Role-based views

Different dashboards for different roles. Plant owners see generation and savings. O&M teams see alerts and work orders. EPC managers see everything.

Live alerts

Instant alerts when plant performance drops or equipment fails. No more manual checks, no more late discoveries.

Work orders & assignment

Create work orders from alerts. Assign to specific team members. Track status until resolved. Every alert gets an owner.

Activity history

Complete history of alerts, work orders, and resolutions. Audit trail for compliance. Learn from past issues.

Plant-level monitoring

Monitor multiple plants from one dashboard. Fleet-wide visibility. Spot underperforming plants instantly.

Performance reporting

Automated performance reports. Generation vs targets, downtime analysis, maintenance logs — all at your fingertips.
