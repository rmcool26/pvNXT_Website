Overview

What is pvNXT Go?

pvNXT Go is the installer field portal for solar projects. Site teams can track tasks, capture daily progress with photos, update status in real-time, and flag delays with reasons — all from their mobile devices on the rooftop. Built for field conditions, not office desks.

What it does

Field operations, streamlined.

Site task assignment

Assign tasks to site teams with clear instructions, deadlines, and checklists. Everyone knows what to do and when.

Daily photo proof

Capture and upload daily site photos as progress proof. Visual timeline of work completed, accessible to office teams.

Status updates

Update project status from the field in real-time. Office teams see progress as it happens, no need to wait for end-of-day reports.

Delay-reason tracking

Flag delays with reasons (weather, material, labor) as they happen. Proactive alerts help office teams adjust timelines.

Verification

Office teams verify field updates and photos. Quality checks built into the workflow, not as an afterthought.

Multi-site dashboard

Manage multiple sites from one dashboard. See all ongoing projects, their status, and team assignments at a glance.
